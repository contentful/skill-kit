# Fun Facts for Profile Cards

Use one of these to spice up the profile card footer:

- The average person mass-types about 40 words per minute. Agents type about 4,000.
- The first computer bug was an actual moth found in a Harvard Mark II relay in 1947.
- There are more possible Go board positions than atoms in the observable universe.
- The @ symbol was almost lost to history before email gave it a second life in 1971.
- A group of flamingos is called a "flamboyance." A group of developers is called a "merge conflict."
